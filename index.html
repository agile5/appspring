Appalachian Spring is a former farmhouse situated in the heart of Dartington near Totnes in Devon. With a mature south-facing garden, private patio for guests, and located just 1 mile from Totnes rail station, Appalachian Spring B&B makes the ideal Bed & Breakfast for your home away from home.

5* Rating on Tripadvisor

After undergoing extensive renovation to bring the continuously occupied 200 year-old farmhouse up to luxury Bed & Breakfast standards, Appalachian Spring opened its doors in the Spring of 2012. Since then it has received overwhelmingly positive reviews, and currently holds a coveted 5* rating on Tripadvisor.
